The Vision

Redefining Energy.

MINT Speciality was born from a singular obsession: to merge the uncompromising quality of third-wave coffee with the velocity of the modern world.

We don't just sell drinks. We craft high-performance tools for those who refuse to settle.

Crafted for the Modern World

Based in the vibrant coast of Málaga, we looked at the traditional coffee landscape and saw a compromise. You either had the time to sit and wait, or you had to endure the synthetic aftertaste of mass-produced RTDs.

MINT is the answer. 18-hour slow cold extraction. Ceremonial grade matcha. Pure, clean energy in a format that complements your lifestyle—whether you're in a high-rise in Madrid or a studio in Berlin.

Luxury Commodity.

Designed as a statement for your workspace. Crafted as a standard for your performance.
